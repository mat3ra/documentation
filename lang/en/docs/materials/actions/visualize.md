# Visualize Sets

[Sets]({{ reference_url }}/entities-general/sets/) of material entries can be visualized by selecting the `Visualize` option <i class="zmdi zmdi-open-in-browser zmdi-hc-border"></i> under the [actions drop-down menu](../../entities-general/ui/explorer.md#actions-dropdown) to the right of the set's entry within the materials [collection]({{ reference_url }}/accounts/collections/), as viewed under the [Explorer Interface](../../entities-general/ui/explorer.md) of our platform.

This action allows the user to inspect the entire content of the set within a single instance of the [Materials Viewer interface](../ui/viewer.md).
