# Open / Close Remote Desktop

The [Remote Desktop](../remote-desktop.md) connection method for accessing our platform is accessible under the corresponding `Remote Desktop` option available in the [Account Menu menu]({{ interface_url }}/ui/account-menu/) of the [Web Interface]({{ interface_url }}/ui/overview/).

The Remote Desktop can subsequently be closed by clicking the ✕ button at its top-right corner, which reverts the entire screen to its previous appearance.
